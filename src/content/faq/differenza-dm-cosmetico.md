  q: "What is the difference between the Bio RIGENERA® medical devices and the Anti-Age product?"
  a: "Dental, Cicatren, Dermal and Pet are CE medical devices (class IIa/IIb): they act on precise clinical indications listed in the IFUs. The Anti-Age product is instead a cosmetic: it improves the appearance of the skin, with no therapeutic purpose."
